About Us

Welcome to My Restaurant , where great taste meets warm hospitality. Since opening our doors, we’ve been dedicated to serving freshly prepared, flavorful Pakistani and continental dishes in a cozy, welcoming atmosphere. Our recipes are crafted with the finest local ingredients, ensuring every bite is full of freshness and authenticity.

We’re proud to have hosted talented chefs, including Chef Saadat Sadique, who worked with us for a short time and brought his signature touch to our kitchen. From our famous Handi Biryani to our sizzling Fish Tikka, every dish is made with passion to give you a memorable dining experience.
